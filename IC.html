Foodservice Australia - The Industry Event

Melbourne Exhibition Centre - 23 - 25 June 2019

Things attended :

  • The National Restaurant Conference
  • The Sweet spot
  • Aged care summit
  • Café school
  • The Chef of the Year competition
  • The Specialty Producers Pavilion

About :
The National Restaurant Conference gave the opportunity to hear from industry leaders and experts on how to improve your business. The sweet spot demonstration stage had expert presenters showcasing all things sweet - chocolate, gelato and patisserie. Hosted by Nerida Conway, The Sweet Spot included hourly demonstrations exploring the latest trends and techniques. The Aged Care Catering Summit provided expert speakers to help stay informed and to make good decisions. The demands and expectations of Australia’s booming aged care sector are evolving and we are see huge changes in the way meals are prepared and served. At the Aged Care Catering Summit the industry came together to discuss the latest trends and techniques.

Michael Cole was named Chef of the Year 2019. The Specialty Producers Pavilion is an exciting feature located within the Foodservice Australia exhibition. It showcases boutique food, drink and ingredient suppliers from all over Australia

Fine Food Australia Trade Exhibition

Melbourne Exhibition Centre 10/09/2018-13//09/2018

Things attended :

  • Hospitality Trade Exhibition
  • Explored products from over 1,000 exhibiting brands showcasing: general & specialty food, catering equipment, hospitality equipment, bakery, drinks, coffee, tea, dairy, meat & seafood, retail & technology, fitout & design, packaging, natural, organic & free-from, and on-premise liquor.
  • Discovered new tastes for your menu, and find new and innovative brands to stock on shelves.

Fine Food Australia is so much more than just a trade exhibition. It’s a four day celebration of new ideas, innovations and techniques, and the perfect opportunity to see first-hand what’s next for the food industry. Learnt from food industry leaders and successful operators covering practical solutions to common food business issues, including staffing, profitability and operating efficiencies. Gain insight into the top trends affecting Australia’s food industry to ensure the business is in step with changing consumer demands.

A visit to Fine Food Australia is the best way to bring new ideas back to your business and keep your customers coming back for more.
